Foldy

Foldy is a simplified mobile first responsive grid. It's based on the 960 Grid, and is designed for people (like us at Paravel) who are transitioning into Responsive Web Design.

It's ugly on purpose. It's not a framework or a drop-in solution, it's a starting point. It's a learning tool launch pad that is made to be customzied.
